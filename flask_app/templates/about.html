--About Us--

Our Story

Many of us in leadership at Car Wash Supreme grew up watching F1, Nascar, and endurance racing . We watched Caroll Shelby become a racing legend as the first American to win the 24 Hours of Le Mans.

The Car Wash Supreme colors are representative of our founder's very own Honda Civic Type R. As a team of high-achievers, competitors and all-around go-getters, we bring an extra level of determination and drive to the way we run our business. We want to come in first no matter what car washes we're competing with, providing the highest-quality and most efficient washes in the state.

Why Choose Us?

Quality Service Near You

We strive to put our best foot forward and provide quality car to your car. It's our attention to the small details that make us different from the rest. We pride ourselves on making an efficient use of time and making sure you can get your car back as soon as possible.

Caring

We care for all our employees, customers, and especially your cars. We work together as a team to deliver the best car wash experience.

Striving to Improve

Although we offer quality service, we that are striving to learn more everyday to help further deliver a better experience. We are never complacent.

Our Mission

To give customers the most compelling car wash experience possible.

Our Vision

To be the state's best car wash company by enhancing the lives of staff and customers.
